# Australian Budget Wrapped Project Brief

## Product Promise

Enter your taxable income. We estimate your Commonwealth income tax and show an illustrative receipt of Australian Government spending.

## Product Framing

Australian Budget Wrapped is a mobile-first wrapped-style web app for Australian users. A user enters taxable income, receives an estimated Commonwealth income tax amount, and sees an illustrative proportional allocation across Australian Government spending categories.

The app must be clear that this is not exactly where a person's dollars went. It is an illustrative proportional allocation of estimated Commonwealth income tax across Australian Government expenses.

## Privacy Rule

Income must remain client-side only. Do not store income in localStorage, sessionStorage, cookies, a database, analytics, or server logs.

Do not add analytics.

## Initial Setup Slice

This slice creates the Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Vitest, and Playwright scaffold with placeholder pages only:

- `/`
- `/methodology`
- `/sources`
- `/privacy`

The full wrapped flow, tax engine, allocation engine, charts, sharing, and final source/methodology detail are future tickets.

## Design Rule

Do not copy Tax Wrapped's exact name, UI, copy, layout, animations, colours, illustrations, or assets. Australian Budget Wrapped must have its own visual identity.
